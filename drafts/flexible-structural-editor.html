Thoughts about structural editors

When opening a tag, parenthesis etc. it should not necessarily insert the closing tag or parenthesis, as this can be annoing when one wants to wrap some contents with a tag. Instead, it should show a stack of unclosed tags, so that the user can move his cursor to the point where he wants to close the recently opened tag, and close it there. The stack serves as a visual indicator of what tags are unclosed, and what would be the effect of closing them now, e.g.: 
<article>
  <p>
    <ul>…</ul> and 30 other closed tags
  </p> missing
</article>
  </p> ← cursor here
In the above example, if the closing </p> tag is inserted, then the diagram becomes: 
<article>
  <p>
    <ul>…</ul> and 30 other closed tags
  </p> missing
</article>
  <p> missing ← cursor here
  </p>
